All About Netflix!

Netflix was made in 29 August 1997, Scotts Valley, California. In 1997 Hastings conceived the idea of a subscription-based movie-rental service, after he incurred a large late fee when he failed to return a store-rented videocassette. Netflix was made by Reed Hastings.
